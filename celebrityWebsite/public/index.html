ABOUT

Chew Soowei is a Singapore-based live show-host with more than a decade of experience on stage. She is a well-known figure and sought-after female show-host in the media and entertainment industry. Her first foray and passion is “Radio”, a medium she devoted herself to since the very first live radio show she presented for Radio Heatwave, Ngee Ann Polytechnic’s campus radio station.

After graduating, she dived right into the industry with a morning show on the former SPH channel, WKRZ, now 913fm. The next station that she worked for gained her more experience in the business as she helmed a double post in Safra Radio, hosting an afternoon time-belt and juggling the music responsibility as a music programmer for Power98fm. She eventually made a change to Mediacorp’s 987fm.

Soowei is currently teaching “Radio” as a subject at her alma mater, Ngee Ann Polytechnic amidst numerous side projects like blogging and content development for various media academies. She is a fulltime freelance emcee, part-time Brazilian jiu jitsu, muay thai and boxing enthusiast and part-time travel-thrill adventure seeker.
